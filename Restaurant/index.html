Welcome to SMP Food

Where Every Plate Tells a Story

Founded in 1998 by the Murugesan family in the heart of Chennai, SMP Food Restaurant was built on one belief: that great food is an act of love. From our humble tiffin stall to a 200-seat dining destination, every recipe still carries the soul of our grandmother's kitchen.

We source our spices fresh from Chettinad, our coconuts from Palakkad, and our seafood directly from Kasimedu harbour every morning. No shortcuts. No compromises.
