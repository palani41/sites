Our Story

Three Generations of Culinary Love

From a roadside tiffin cart in 1998 to Chennai's most beloved multi-cuisine restaurant — this is how it happened.

Who We Are

SMP Food Restaurant — A Family Legacy

SMP stands for Senguttuvan Murugesan Palanisamy — the three brothers who started this restaurant with ₹12,000 in savings and an unshakeable belief that Chennai deserved better food.

Today, that belief has grown into a 200-seat restaurant, a team of 60 passionate staff, and a menu spanning seven cuisines — all without losing the warmth and soul of that original roadside cart.

Every morning starts the same way it always has: our head chef lights the first fire at 5:30 AM, and the first batch of idli batter goes on to steam. Some things never change.

The Journey

Our Story So Far

1998
The Cart Begins

Brothers Senguttuvan, Murugesan and Palanisamy set up a tiffin cart near Vadapalani junction with ₹12,000 and their mother's recipes. Sold 200 idlis on day one.

🍛
🏠
2003
First Restaurant

Opened a 40-seat restaurant on Anna Salai. Added North Indian and Chinese to the menu. Hired the first professional chef from IHM Chennai.

2010
Seafood & Expansion

Partnered directly with Kasimedu harbour fishermen. Launched our legendary Crab Masala. Expanded to 120 seats. Won first Tamil Nadu Tourism Award.

🦞
🏆
2018
Grand Renovation

Complete renovation to 200 seats. Private dining rooms, live tandoor station, bar. Named Chennai's Best Multi-Cuisine Restaurant by The Hindu.

2024
Today

100+ dishes, 60 staff, 25,000+ happy guests, and the same grandmother's idli recipe that started it all. The cart became a landmark.

What Drives Us

Mission, Vision & Core Values

🎯

Our Mission

To serve authentic, freshly prepared food that honours regional culinary traditions while offering a warm, welcoming environment where every guest feels like family — regardless of occasion or budget.

🔭

Our Vision

To become South India's most trusted family dining destination — a place where recipes are preserved, communities are nourished, and the art of cooking is celebrated as the highest form of hospitality.

🌿
Freshness Always

No frozen shortcuts. No pre-made pastes. Everything starts from scratch, every single day.

🤝
Honest Hospitality

We treat every guest the way we'd want our own family to be treated — with warmth, respect and genuine care.

🏘️
Community Roots

We employ locally, source locally, and give back locally. Our neighbourhood made us who we are.

📜
Recipe Integrity

Our recipes are documented, respected and only changed with deep intention. Tradition is not a constraint — it's our compass.
